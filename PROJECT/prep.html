Preparation For Donation

Pre-Donation Care

  • Drink plenty of water in the 24 hours leading up to your donation.
  • Have a light meal before donating, avoiding fatty foods.
  • Get a good night's sleep before the day of donation.
  • Avoid alcohol and caffeine on the day of donation.
  • If you have any questions, consult with the blood donation staff.

Post-Donation Care

  • Rest for at least 10-15 minutes after your donation.
  • Have a snack and drink water or juice to replenish your energy.
  • Avoid heavy exercise for the rest of the day.
  • Keep the bandage on for at least a few hours.
  • If you feel dizzy or faint, lie down with your feet elevated until you feel better.
